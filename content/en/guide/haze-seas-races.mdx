<Section icon={Target} title="The Path to V2 Awakening">

Transitioning from V1 to V2 is the most significant power spike a player can achieve in Haze Seas. This process is not a simple purchase; it involves a rigorous set of challenges that test your combat skills and patience. You cannot begin this journey until you have reached the level cap requirements, ensuring that only experienced players can access these evolved forms.

The V2 questline is standardized across all races, meaning whether you are a Human or a member of the DClan, you must prove your worth through the same trials.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Level Requirement">
    You must reach **Level 2200** before the V2 Quest NPC will interact with you. Attempting to start earlier will result in the NPC ignoring your request.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Sea Beast Hunt">
    The first trial requires you to locate and defeat a **Sea Beast**. These spawn in open water and require decent ship durability or high-range Fruit abilities to take down safely.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Scavenger Hunt">
    Collect **50 Chests** scattered across the various islands of the map. This is best completed by visiting high-density areas like Starter Island or Marine Base.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Zenith Boss Confrontation">
    Defeat the **Zenith Boss**. This is a high-level encounter that rewards you with the quest completion and a chance to drop a unique sword.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Final Payment">
    After completing the combat trials, you must pay **1,000,000 Belly** and **100 Gems** to the NPC to finalize the awakening.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Quest Persistence">
Once you unlock V2 for one race, the quest can be repeated if you switch races. However, you will need to pay the Belly and Gem costs again for each new race you wish to awaken.
</Callout>

</Section>
