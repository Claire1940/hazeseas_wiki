<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Leveling Guide">

To transition from a beginner to a pro, you must follow a structured progression path. Haze Seas rewards players who move between islands as soon as they hit the level requirements. Staying at a low-level island for too long will result in diminishing EXP returns.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Claim Initial Codes">
    Open the Menu, select the Treasure Chest icon, and enter all active codes. This gives you the initial capital to buy a **Katana** or a **Black Leg** style.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="First Sea Island Hopping">
    Start at Starter Island, move to Shark Park at Level 120, and reach Desert Ruins by Level 200. Always pick up the local quest before fighting the boss.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Secure a Logia Fruit">
    Try to obtain an elemental (Logia) fruit like **Flame, Light, or Magma**. These provide "Logia Reflex," making you immune to physical attacks from lower-level NPCs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Farm Boss Drops">
    Focus on bosses like the **Thunder God** (Level 1100) for the Golden Staff or **Ryummy** (Level 2150) for the Shusui sword.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Transition to Second Sea">
    Once you surpass Level 2000 and complete the necessary quests, head to the Second Sea to unlock **Advanced Fighting Styles** like Dragon Claw.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Tip">
When farming bosses, try to group up with other players. Even if you don't deal the most damage, participating in the kill grants you full EXP and a chance at the rare drop loot table.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Devil Fruits and Spawn Mechanics">

Devil Fruits are the cornerstone of the Haze Seas meta. Understanding how they spawn and which ones to keep is vital for your **haze seas noob to pro guide** knowledge base. There are currently over 33 fruits in the game, categorized by rarity: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Legendary, and Mythical.

<Callout type="warning" title="Despawn Alert">
Fruits spawn every **60 minutes** in public servers. However, they will disappear (despawn) after only **20 minutes** if no one picks them up. Keep a timer running!
</Callout>

| Fruit Name | Rarity | Best For | Key Ability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dough** | **Mythical** | PvP / Bossing | High combo potential and stuns. |
| **Dragon** | **Mythical** | Endgame Tanking | Massive transformation health buff. |
| **Magma** | Rare | Sea Events | Highest DPS for water-based combat. |
| **Light** | Rare | Travel / Grinding | Fastest flight speed in the game. |
| **Chop** | Common | Sword Immunity | Best starter fruit for sword bosses. |

**Fruit Stock and VIP Servers:**
- **Fruit Stock**: Rotates in the shop every few hours. Check the `#fruit-stock` channel in the official Discord to see if a Legendary fruit is available for purchase.
- **VIP Servers**: These allow for a limit of **5 fruit spawns** at a time. This is the safest way to hunt for fruits without competition from other players.
