<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Second Sea Unlock Quest">

To begin the journey to the Second Sea, you must complete a short puzzle quest involving the Dimensional Sailor and a hidden ancient artifact. This quest tests your mobility and traversal skills, requiring you to navigate the vertical cliffs of the Rocky Pillars.

Make sure you have unlocked both **Sky Walk** and **Flash Step** before starting. These movement abilities are mandatory for reaching the hidden cave where the quest item is kept. Without them, phasing through the secret barriers is mathematically impossible.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Dimensional Sailor">
    Travel to the northern docks of the Starter Island in the First Sea. Look for the NPC named the Dimensional Sailor standing near the sailboats. Speak with him to receive a quest to locate a lost Road Poneglyph copy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Sail to the Rocky Pillars">
    Spawn a boat or use a flying transformation fruit to travel northeast from the Starter Island. The Rocky Pillars are a cluster of tall stone columns rising directly out of the ocean, easily visible from the starter docks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Climb the Flagged Pillar">
    Locate the central pillar that features a small wooden platform and a flag. Land your boat at the base of this pillar and prepare your movement abilities for a vertical climb.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Phase Into the Hidden Cave">
    Climb up the left side of the flagged pillar using Sky Walk. Once you reach the fourth rocky ridge, look for a dark, recessed wall. Position your character against this wall and use Flash Step to teleport directly through the collision barrier into a hidden inner chamber.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Retrieve the Poneglyph Scroll">
    Inside the hidden chamber, locate the black stone column known as the Poneglyph. Approach it and hold the interact key to copy the ancient writings, adding the Poneglyph Scroll directly into your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Return and Teleport">
    Flash Step back out of the pillar and return to the Starter Island. Speak with the Dimensional Sailor once more, hand over the scroll, and accept his offer to sail to Wano. The game will load you directly into the Second Sea hub.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Flash Step Requirement">
If you do not have Flash Step, you can purchase it from the Trainer on Clown Island for 50,000 Beli. It is an absolute necessity for passing through the stone pillar wall.
</Callout>

</Section>
