<Section icon={Swords} title="Late First Sea & Legendary Drop Routes">

The final stretch of the First Sea (Levels 1400-2150) introduces bosses with complex AI and massive health pools. Farming **Ryummy** at level 2150 is the ultimate goal for sword users, as he drops the legendary **Shusui**. During this phase, you should also be tracking **Devil Fruit spawn timers** (60 minutes) to ensure you have a high-tier fruit like **Dough** or **Venom** to handle the high DPS requirements of the Thriller Boat.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prepare for Impel Jail">
    Reach level 1400 and challenge the Warden. Secure the **Warden Hat** for stamina regeneration, which is vital for the upcoming Fishman Island travel.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Fishman Island Farming">
    At level 1700, challenge Neptune. You are looking for the **Neptune Crown** and the **Fork** weapon. This area is excellent for leveling due to high NPC density.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Shusui Grind">
    Enter the Thriller Boat at level 2150. Ryummy is a difficult fight; use ranged attacks to avoid his sword combos. Repeat until **Shusui** drops.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Boss | Level | Key Drop | Best Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Warden** | 1400 | Warden Hat | Stamina / Speed builds |
| **Neptune** | 1700 | Neptune Crown | Health / Sea Resistance |
| **Shiryu** | 1850 | Raiu Scroll | Fast Sword pressure |
| **G4 Boss** | 2000 | Gear 4 Book | Transformation unlock |
| **Ryummy** | 2150 | Shusui | Late-game Sword meta |

<Callout type="success" title="Advanced Strategy">
For the G4 Boss, ensure you have maximized your **Observation Haki**. His attacks are fast and hit multiple times, making manual dodging nearly impossible without Haki assistance.
</Callout>
