<Section icon={Swords} title="Buso Haki (Armament) Unlock & Progression">

Buso Haki, also known as Armament Haki, coats your character's limbs or weapons in a dark, metallic armor. This spiritual armor significantly increases your damage output and allows you to strike the physical bodies of Logia users. Buso Haki features two distinct progression tiers, each requiring you to locate the legendary trainer, Old Man Silver, on different islands across the sea.

<Section icon={Map} title="Observation Haki (Kenbunshoku) Unlock & Progression">

Observation Haki, or Kenbunshoku Haki, allows your character to sense the presence of nearby enemies and automatically dodge incoming physical attacks. This utility is incredibly valuable for dodging boss ultimate moves and surviving high-pressure PvP encounters.

When activated by pressing the **R** key, Observation Haki applies a bright outline to all nearby players and NPCs, allowing you to track their movements even through solid walls or during thick fog. This is exceptionally useful in Haze Seas, where visibility can sometimes be obscured by weather effects or dense island foliage.

Furthermore, Observation Haki has unique interactions with certain character races. For example, if you are a member of the legendary **D. Clan** race or the legendary **Dragonoid** race, you receive a passive bonus of `+1 Observation Haki` dodge. This means a D. Clan member utilizing Observation Haki V2 can execute 6 consecutive dodges instead of the standard 5, providing a massive edge in competitive PvP duels.

| Level | Requirements | Cost | Trainer NPC | Location | Dodges |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Observation V1** | Level 600 | 150,000 Beli | Observation Trainer | Marine Base | 3 Dodges (Base) |
| **Observation V2** | Level 1,500 | 200,000 Beli | Observation Trainer | Bubble Island | 5 Dodges (Base) |

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Conqueror's Haki (Haoshoku) Unlocking Methods">

Conqueror's Haki, or Haoshoku Haki, is the rarest and most powerful Haki type in the game. Unlike Buso and Observation, which can be purchased directly from trainers by any player who meets the level requirement, Conqueror's Haki is locked behind specific premium or highly rare progression paths.

When activated by pressing the **Z** key, Conqueror's Haki unleashes a massive spiritual shockwave that instantly stuns and intimidates all nearby enemies who are at a lower level than your character. This makes it the ultimate tool for clearing out large clusters of low-level mobs during grinding sessions, allowing you to group them up and defeat them without taking a single hit.

However, players must note that Conqueror's Haki does not affect boss-level NPCs or players who are at a higher level than you. It is purely designed as a crowd-control mechanic for trash mobs and an intimidation tool in PvP.

| Method | Requirements | Cost | Rarity/Rate | Primary Effect |

| **Gamepass Purchase** | Buy Conqueror Spirit | 2,500 Robux | Guaranteed | Unlocks Conqueror's Haki instantly on any race |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Advanced Haki Tips & Combat Synergy">

To truly master Haki in Haze Seas, you must understand how to weave it into your combat rotations. Haki is not a standalone system; it is designed to complement your primary damage stats (Strength, Sword, or Devil Fruit). If you are running a high-damage Sword build using legendary blades like *Enma* or *3 Sword Style*, Buso Haki V2 is non-negotiable. The 7.5% damage multiplier applies directly to your base M1 attacks, allowing you to melt boss health bars in record time.

For Devil Fruit mains, particularly those using fragile glass-cannon fruits like *Pika (Light)* or *Magma*, Observation Haki acts as your primary safety net. Since your focus is dealing massive ranged damage from a distance, having 5 passive dodges ensures that if a boss or PvP opponent closes the gap, you can safely retreat or reposition without getting caught in a devastating combo.

| Build Type | Recommended Haki | Primary Synergy | Combat Strategy |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Sword Specialist** | Buso V2 & Observation V2 | Boosts raw sword M1 damage, safe close-range trades | Engage with Buso active, use Observation dodges to counter boss parries |
| **Fruit Main (Logia)** | Observation V2 | Safety net when elemental immunity is bypassed | Keep Observation active to dodge enemy Buso-infused attacks |
| **Melee/Fighting Style** | Buso V2 & Conqueror's | Maximizes close-quarters pressure, stuns group mobs | Group enemies with Conqueror's stun, then unleash Black Leg or Electro combos |

<Callout type="success" title="Mastery Synergy">
Haki damage scaling stacks multiplicatively with your weapon mastery levels. Prioritize leveling up your sword or fighting style mastery alongside your Haki upgrades to unlock maximum damage potential.
</Callout>
