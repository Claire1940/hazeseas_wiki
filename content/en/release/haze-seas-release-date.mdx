<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Devil Fruit System: Mechanics and Roster">

Devil Fruits are the cornerstone of any pirate build in **Haze Seas**. Whether you are a Logia user seeking elemental immunity or a Zoan user looking for raw physical power, understanding the spawn mechanics is vital. In the 2026 rework, the fruit roster has expanded to 34 unique abilities, each with specific mastery paths and potential awakenings.

<Callout type="warning" title="Spawn Rules">
Fruits spawn every **60 minutes** on a server. However, they will despawn after **20 minutes** if no player claims them. In VIP servers, there is a strict **5-fruit limit** to prevent excessive resource hoarding.
</Callout>

### Fruit Rarity and Types
The fruit system is divided into Paramecia, Logia, and Zoan types. Paramecia fruits offer unique utility, Logia fruits provide elemental dodges against non-Haki users, and Zoan fruits focus on powerful transformations.

| Rarity | Examples | Best Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Mythical** | Dough, Dragon | Endgame PvP and high-tier bossing |
| **Legendary** | Phoenix, Leopard, Venom, Gum | Balanced progression and mobility |
| **Rare** | Magma, Light, Ice, Flame | Efficient grinding and early farming |
| **Uncommon** | Smoke, Sand, Barrier | Transitional early-game power |
| **Common** | Chop, Spin, Bomb | Starter utility and basic damage |

For players focused on speed, the **Light** fruit remains a top-tier choice due to its high-speed flight and ranged attacks. For those focused on raw damage, **Magma** provides the highest area-of-effect (AoE) damage for clearing groups of NPCs.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Progression Guide: Island and Boss Roadmap">

Progressing in Haze Seas requires a strategic route through the various islands. Each island features a level-appropriate boss that drops essential gear or materials. Following the correct roadmap ensures you are never under-leveled for the next major challenge.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Starter Islands to Shark Park">
    Begin by grinding basic NPCs until level 120. Head to **Shark Park** to farm the Shark Boss for the **Shark Blade**, your first reliable rare sword.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mid-Game Marine HQ and Sky Islands">
    Reach level 900 at Marine HQ to challenge the Ice Admiral for the **Sleeping Mask**. Then, move to the Sky Islands (Level 1100) to farm the Thunder God for the **Golden Staff**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Late-Game Thriller Boat and Beyond">
    At level 2150, the Thriller Boat becomes your primary farming spot. Defeat Ryummy for the **Shusui** sword and the Blue Scarf accessory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Second Sea Transition">
    Once you surpass the level 2500 threshold, prepare for the Second Sea. Focus on advanced styles like **Cyborg** or **Dragonic** to handle the increased difficulty.
  </Step>
</Steps>
