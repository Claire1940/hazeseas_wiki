Obtaining a legendary weapon is only half the battle [homepage-outline]. To truly dominate the high-level seas, you must optimize your gear through the upgrade system [homepage-outline]. Upgrading your weapons to their "V2" versions unlocks enhanced skill sets, increased damage scaling, and visually stunning effects [homepage-modules-2].

The upgrade process requires players to gather rare materials scattered across the islands [homepage-outline, homepage-modules-2]. For example, upgrading the Sea Beast Hammer to its V2 counterpart requires defeating the formidable White Sea Beast [homepage-modules-2]. Similarly, upgrading the Bisento to Bisento V2 requires defeating Tremor Girl, a special super boss with a 20% drop rate for the upgrade [homepage-modules-2].

The Blacksmith NPC, located on specific late-game islands, is your go-to destination for these upgrades. You will need to bring him the base weapon along with the required materials and a substantial amount of Cash.

Farming these materials can be time-consuming. Lava Ore can be obtained by defeating NPCs in Marine Base Town (level 650+) [homepage-modules-2]. Ice Ore is farmed from the Cold Island Lab Route (level 1550+) [homepage-modules-2]. Sea Beast Cores are rare drops from the standard Sea Beast event at Rocky Pillars [homepage-modules-2].
