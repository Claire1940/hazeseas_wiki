<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock Advanced Sword Styles">

Beyond single-blade combat, Haze Seas offers specialized sword styles that change your entire move set. These are not just weapons but complete combat overhauls that require specific NPC interactions and significant amounts of Beli.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Basic Katana">
    Purchase the standard Katana from the Sword Shop on the starter island to begin your journey.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock 2 Sword Style">
    Travel to the appropriate NPC (Mr. White) and pay 100,000 Money to unlock the base 2 Sword Style.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Upgrade to 2SS V2">
    Defeat the Dual Swordsman at Logue City (Lvl 500) for a 10% chance to drop the upgrade materials/book for V2.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reach 3 Sword Style">
    Find the 3SS Boss in the late-game boss route. This is a 5% drop and represents one of the highest DPS sword paths in the game.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Style Synergy">
Sword styles often have better combo potential than individual weapons, making them preferred for high-level PvP encounters.
</Callout>
