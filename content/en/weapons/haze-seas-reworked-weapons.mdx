<Section icon={Target} title="Steps to Upgrade Weapons to V2">

Several mid-game weapons can be upgraded to their highly powerful "V2" forms. Upgrading your weapons is essential for transitioning into late-game content, as it introduces enhanced visual effects, wider hitboxes, and significantly higher damage scaling.

The upgrade process typically requires you to first own the V1 version of the weapon, reach a specific level, and then defeat a super boss or complete a dedicated quest line.

<Callout type="success" title="Upgrade Success">
Upgrading a weapon to V2 does not reset your Weapon Mastery level. You will retain all accumulated experience, allowing you to immediately use the upgraded skills if you meet the mastery requirements.
</Callout>

Follow these steps to successfully upgrade your weapons to their V2 forms:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire the Base V1 Weapon">
    Before attempting any upgrade, you must obtain the baseline weapon. For example, purchase 2 Sword Style from Mr. White for 100,000 money, or defeat the Mace Boss on Winter Island to secure the standard Mace.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Reach the Required Level & Island">
    Ensure your character meets the level requirements for the target upgrade boss. For 2 Sword Style V2, you must travel to Logue City and prepare to face the Dual Swordsman (Level 500). For Bisento V2, prepare to face Tremor Girl.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defeat the Super Boss for the Upgrade Item">
    Engage and defeat the specified super boss. These bosses have enhanced health pools and utilize advanced movesets. Defeating them grants a chance to drop either the direct V2 weapon or the scroll required to unlock it.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm the Upgrade and Allocate Stats">
    Once the V2 weapon drops, equip it from your inventory. To maximize its effectiveness, use a stat reset code if necessary to reallocate points from Fruit or Combat directly into your Weapon stat.
  </Step>
</Steps>
