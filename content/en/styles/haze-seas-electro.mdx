To unlock this style efficiently, players should follow a structured path through the First Sea to ensure they have the levels and currency required to survive the Udon Prison environment.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Leveling to the Threshold">
    Before heading to Udon Prison, ensure you are at least **Level 1100**. While the island can be visited earlier, the NPCs in the area are aggressive and can easily overwhelm under-leveled players.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Currency Accumulation">
    The Electro style is a **money purchase route**. Unlike the Golden Staff or Enma, you do not need to rely on a low-percentage drop rate. Farm bosses like the **Thunder God** at Sky Islands or the **Warden** at Impel Jail to reach the required funds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Navigating to Udon Prison">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Interacting with the Trainer">

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Skill Comparisons">

The **haze seas electro** skillset is designed around the concept of "Shock and Awe." By combining rapid strikes with electrical discharges, players can maintain a constant flow of damage while keeping their targets stationary.

Compared to other fighting styles available in the rework, Electro offers a middle ground between the accessibility of Black Leg and the raw power of Dragonic.

| Fighting Style | Tier | Primary Benefit | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dragonic** | **S** | Extreme Damage | Late-game PvP / Bossing |
| **Electro** | **A** | **Speed & Stun** | **Fast Grinding / PvP Combos** |
| **Fish-Man Karate** | **A** | Balanced Range | Sea Events / PvE |
| **Black Leg** | **B** | Early Upgrades | Mid-game Progression |
| **Combat** | **C** | None | Starter Levels |

**Key Skill Features of Electro:**
- **Electric Dash**: A high-velocity movement skill that damages enemies in the path.
- **Thunder Clap**: A localized AoE (Area of Effect) burst that applies a 2-second stun to all nearby targets.
- **Lightning Barrage**: A flurry of punches infused with electricity, dealing multi-hit damage and preventing enemy health regeneration for a short duration.

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Combo Tip">
Use the **Thunder Clap** stun immediately followed by a high-damage sword skill like **Enma's** slash or a Mythical fruit ability. The stun ensures your most powerful moves never miss their mark.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimizing Your Electro Build">

To truly master **haze seas electro**, you must look beyond the style itself and consider your entire character build. Because Electro relies on speed, your choice of race and secondary weapons will dictate your effectiveness in the Second Sea.

**Recommended Races for Electro Users:**
- **Mink (Uncommon)**: The gold standard for Electro. The walk speed and jump power bonuses stack with Electro's mobility, making you nearly impossible to hit.
- **D. Clan (Mythical)**: Provides massive stamina and health regeneration, allowing you to spam Electro skills without running out of energy.
- **Cyborg (Uncommon)**: Adds extra durability and health regeneration, which is useful for players who prefer a "tanky" melee playstyle.

**Weapon Synergies:**
Since Electro is a fighting style, it occupies your melee slot. You should pair it with weapons that cover its weaknesses—specifically long-range engagement.

| Weapon | Type | Synergy Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Golden Staff** | Legendary Sword | Electricity-linked theme; shares Sky Island origins. |
| **Yoru / Dark Blade** | Mythical Sword | High damage to finish targets stunned by Electro. |
| **Sea Beast Hammer** | Legendary Weapon | Heavy stun stacking for total crowd control. |

<Callout type="info" title="Stat Distribution">
For an Electro-focused build, prioritize **Melee/Strength** and **Defense**. If you are using a Logia fruit for mobility, keep your **Fruit** stats secondary unless you are running a hybrid build.
</Callout>
