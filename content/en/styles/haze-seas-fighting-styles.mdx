<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Style Mechanics & Combat Performance">

Every fighting style in Haze Seas features a unique moveset designed for specific gameplay scenarios. Understanding the strengths and weaknesses of each style allows you to tailor your build to your current goals, whether you are trying to speed-run the leveling process or dominate in arena PvP.

### Combat (Starter Style)
The default style that every pirate begins with. It features basic punch combinations with minimal range and low damage scaling. While it is useful for defeating the first few waves of bandits on Starter Island, you should replace it as soon as you save enough money for an upgrade.

### Black Leg (Progression Melee)
Located at the Sea Restaurant, this kick-based style is the absolute best choice for early-to-mid game progression. Its moveset includes powerful fire-infused attacks that can damage multiple enemies at once. The area-of-effect damage makes it incredibly efficient for clearing grouped quest mobs.

### Electro (Stun Melee)
Taught by a trainer in Udon Prison, Electro focuses on rapid lightning strikes and high-speed mobility. Its standout feature is its ability to stun opponents, making it highly valuable in PvP setups. The quick dashes and stun locks allow you to interrupt enemy skills and set up devastating follow-up attacks.

### Fish-Man Karate (Balanced Melee)
This style blends close-range physical strikes with water-based projectile attacks. Unlocked on Fishman Island, it is one of the most versatile styles in the game. The ranged water blasts allow you to pull enemies from a distance safely, while the heavy physical finishers keep close-range threats at bay.

### Cyborg & Dragonic (Advanced Melee)
These legendary styles represent the absolute pinnacle of melee combat in Haze Seas. Cyborg utilizes high-tech energy blasts and heavy physical impacts to deal massive burst damage, making it a premier boss-killing tool. Dragonic (Dragon Claw) offers unparalleled combo extension and aggressive pressure, allowing skilled players to lock down opponents in PvP without giving them a chance to retaliate.
