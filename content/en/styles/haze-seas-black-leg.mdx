Securing the Black Leg style requires a mix of map navigation and financial preparation. Players must travel to the Sea Restaurant, a prominent landmark in the First Sea, and gather the necessary funds before the trainer will offer to teach the style.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Navigate to the Sea Restaurant">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Accumulate 50,000 Cash">
    Ensure you have at least 50,000 Cash in your inventory. If you are short on funds, farming the local Krieg Boss (Level 300) or completing nearby island quests is a highly efficient way to close the gap.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Black Leg Trainer">
    Climb to the upper decks or look near the designated trainer platforms to locate the Black Leg Trainer NPC. Interact with him to open the purchase prompt.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Purchase the Style & Allocate Stats">
    Confirm the transaction to instantly equip the style. If your character was previously optimized for swords or Devil Fruits, use a stat reset to reallocate points into the Combat stat to maximize your new kick damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To maximize the potential of the Black Leg style, you must optimize your stat points and gear. Spreading your stats too thin across Swords, Fruits, and Combat will result in mediocre damage output across all three systems.

| Stat Name | Priority | Recommended Ratio | Gameplay Benefit |
