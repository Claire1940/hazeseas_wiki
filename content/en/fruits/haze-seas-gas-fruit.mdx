To unlock the full potential of the **haze seas gas fruit**, players must invest in their Fruit stat and accumulate Mastery points. Each ability unlocked offers a different tactical advantage, ranging from localized explosions to massive field-clearing detonations.

### Complete Move Set Table

| Move Name | Key | Mastery | Description |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Gas Zone** | Z | 30 | Places a gaseous sphere that explodes after a short delay, dealing localized damage. |
| **Cremation** | X | 60 | Triggers a burst of high-temperature gas around the user, ideal for clearing surrounding mobs. |
| **Gas Mode** | C | 90 | Grants a powerful utility buff that increases movement speed, jump height, and dash distance. |

| **Iris Detonation** | B | 150 | Charges up a massive explosive blast with an immense AoE radius and devastating damage output. |

### Tactical Analysis of Moves

- **Gas Zone (Z Key):** This is your primary starting move. It allows you to place a sphere at a distance, which quickly detonates. It is excellent for drawing the attention of distant mobs and grouping them together.
- **Cremation (X Key):** Once mobs are grouped around you, Cremation is your go-to response. Because it centers on your character, your Elemental Protection ensures you take minimal damage while the fiery burst decimates the enemies.
- **Gas Mode (C Key):** Unlike some elemental fruits that offer direct flight, the Gas Fruit provides a massive stat boost to your physical mobility. This is perfect for dodging boss skills and navigating tight terrains.
- **Fire Ignition (V Key):** This is a highly unique projectile attack. The cylinder of fire can pass through solid walls and buildings, allowing you to cheese difficult bosses by attacking them from safety.
- **Iris Detonation (B Key):** The ultimate move of this fruit. Although it requires a brief charge-up period, the resulting explosion covers almost the entire screen, dealing massive damage to anything caught inside.
