<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Awakening Your Fruit">

The process of achieving **haze seas fruit awakening** is more complex than simple level grinding. It involves a multi-stage journey through the First and Second Seas, requiring players to defeat powerful bosses and gather rare materials.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Mastery Thresholds">
    Before any awakening NPC will interact with you, you must demonstrate proficiency with your current fruit. Use your abilities against bosses like the **Thunder God** (Level 1100) or **Neptune** (Level 1700) to quickly gain Mastery points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect Awakening Currency">
    Awakening is not free. You will need a significant amount of **Gems** and **Bounty**. Gems are best farmed through **Sea Events** like the Sea Beast at Rocky Pillars or by completing high-level quests in the Second Sea.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defeat the Specific Fruit Boss">
    Many fruits require a "heart" or "scroll" dropped by a specific boss. For example, those pursuing sword-hybrid builds often farm the **Enma Boss** or **3SS Boss** for complementary gear, but fruit users must focus on bosses that share their elemental theme.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Locate the Awakening Trainer">
    Advanced trainers are usually hidden in remote locations like **Udon Prison** or specialized islands in the Second Sea. You must have the base fruit equipped and the required materials in your inventory to initiate the transformation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Risky Swaps">
Replacing your fruit after awakening it may result in losing progress if you haven't permanently unlocked the slot. Always check the **official Discord #fruit-stock** channel before making a decision to switch your build.
</Callout>
