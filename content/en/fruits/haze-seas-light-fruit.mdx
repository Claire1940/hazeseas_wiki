<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering the Haze Seas Light Fruit Abilities">

Unlocking the full potential of this fruit requires grinding Mastery points. Unlike physical weapons, elemental fruits scale directly with your Fruit stat, making it crucial to allocate your attribute points correctly. The passive ability, Elemental Protection, grants complete immunity to standard melee and sword attacks from NPCs and players who do not have Busoshoku Haki active. This makes early-game farming incredibly safe, as low-level mobs cannot deal any damage to you.

| Ability Name | Key | Mastery | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Elemental Protection** | Passive | 0 | Immune to physical/sword attacks unless attacker has Haki |
| **Illumination** | Z Key | 30 | Fires a light ball that explodes at cursor location |
| **Light Sword** | X Key | 60 | Summons a solid sword of light for melee slashing attacks |
| **Light Fly** | C Key | 90 | Transforms into a beam of light for high-speed flight |
| **Light Beam** | V Key | 120 | Emits a concentrated laser beam dealing continuous damage |
| **Sacred Jewels** | B Key | 150 | Rains down multiple explosive light projectiles from the sky |

As you increase your mastery, the damage and utility of these skills grow exponentially. The Light Sword (X Key) is particularly notable because it allows fruit-focused builds to engage in close-quarters combat without splitting stat points into the Sword attribute, as its damage scales directly with your primary Fruit stat.

<Callout type="tip" title="Stat Allocation Tip">
For elemental builds, allocate 90% of your stat points into the Fruit attribute, and split the remaining 10% evenly between Defense and Combat. This maximizes your damage output while keeping you alive.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Mastery Farming Route">

Leveling up your mastery quickly is essential to unlock the high-tier AoE skills of the **haze seas light fruit**. Follow this optimized progression route to reach 150 Mastery in the shortest time possible.

By concentrating your efforts on bosses rather than standard trash mobs, you can maximize your experience gain. Bosses provide a massive boost to both your character level and fruit mastery, allowing you to unlock skills like Light Fly and Sacred Jewels much faster than standard grinding methods.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip and Level Up on Starter Island">
    Start by defeating low-level Thief and Bandit NPCs. Since you have Elemental Protection, gather them in a tight group and punch them down. Once you unlock Illumination at 30 Mastery, use it to clear groups instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm the Bandit Boss">
    Head to the Bandit Boss (Level 25) on Starter Island. Set your spawn point nearby, accept the quest, and defeat him repeatedly. His high experience yield will quickly push your mastery to 60, unlocking the Light Sword.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Transition to Logue Town & Skypeia">
    Move to higher-level islands like Logue Town and Skypeia. Use your Light Fly ability to travel instantly. Focus on farming boss NPCs rather than standard mobs, as bosses give significantly more mastery and Beli.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Server-Hop Bosses in Sea 2">
    Once you reach level 700 and enter the Second Sea, server-hop bosses while taking their respective quests. This method yields millions of Beli and easily maxes out your fruit mastery to 150+.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Haki Users Warning">
Be careful when fighting high-level bosses in the Second Sea. Many of them possess Buso Haki, which bypasses your Elemental Protection and allows them to deal full physical damage to you.
</Callout>

</Section>
