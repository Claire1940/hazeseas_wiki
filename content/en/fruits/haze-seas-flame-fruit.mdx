<Section icon={Target} title="Best PvP Combos & Farming Strategy">

Farming and PvP combat with the Flame Fruit require a deep understanding of positioning, block-breaking, and cooldown management. Because your flight speed is highly dependent on your current stamina, balancing your stat points is crucial.

When farming bosses or large groups of mobs, your goal is to maintain distance while keeping them burning. Use Fire Pillar to interrupt enemy animations, and charge your Flame Emperor from a safe distance before unleashing it onto the spawn point.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate with Flame Emperor">
    Hold down the **B key** to charge your Flame Emperor to its maximum size, then release it towards the enemy to break their guard and deal massive initial AoE damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Deploy Fire Flies for Homing Damage">
    Quickly follow up by pressing the **V key** to launch Fire Flies. These homing projectiles will automatically lock onto the stunned enemy, tracking their movement and delivering rapid burst damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Stun with Fire Pillar">
    While the enemy is dealing with the fireflies, aim your cursor directly beneath them and press the **X key** to summon a massive Fire Pillar, launching them upward and dealing continuous ticking damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Finish with Charged Fire Fist">
    As they fall back down, charge up your Fire Fist by holding the **Z key** and release it directly at their landing spot to secure the final blow.
  </Step>
</Steps>
