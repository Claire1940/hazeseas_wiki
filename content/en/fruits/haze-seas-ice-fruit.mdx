<Section icon={Swords} title="Active and Passive Abilities">

The true strength of the **haze seas ice fruit** lies in its unique passive traits and crowd-controlling active moveset. As an Elemental type fruit, it grants the user passive immunity to basic physical attacks, allowing you to ignore damage from minor island mobs. Additionally, the fruit completely changes how you interact with the game's oceanic environment.

Normally, falling into the sea drains your health and limits your movement. With the Ice fruit active, the water beneath your feet instantly freezes, allowing you to sprint across the sea surface. Furthermore, selecting the fruit allows you to summon an Ice Spear for basic left-click attacks. Even though this weapon looks and behaves like a sword, its damage scales entirely with your Fruit stat, allowing hybrid playstyles without splitting your stat points.

<Callout type="warning" title="Buso Haki Vulnerability">
Do not rely blindly on your physical immunity. Bosses and high-level PvP opponents utilizing Buso Haki can bypass your Elemental Protection and deal full damage.
</Callout>
