<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Gum Fruit Overview and Rarity">

The **haze seas gum fruit** (historically known as the Rubber Fruit or Gomu Gomu no Mi) is one of the most iconic and versatile Legendary Paramecia-type fruits available in the game. Known for its unique blend of high mobility, heavy knockback, and devastating transformation states, this fruit is a highly sought-after asset for both PvE farming and competitive PvP duels. Unlike Logia fruits that offer passive elemental intangibility, the Gum Fruit relies on active damage mitigation, rapid-fire physical strikes, and elastic mobility to overwhelm opponents.

In the 2026 rework of Haze Seas, the developers have fine-tuned the physics engine, making the elastic movement of the Gum Fruit feel incredibly fluid. Players can slingshot across islands, execute rapid mid-air direction changes, and transition seamlessly between base combat and advanced transformations. While it starts as a highly capable progression tool, its true potential is unlocked in the late game when you begin pursuing its advanced Gears.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Gum Fruit Moveset and Combat Mechanics">

The moveset of the Gum Fruit is split across three distinct phases: Base Form, Gear 2, and the mythical Gear 4 Boundman transformation. Each phase completely alters your combat loop, skill cooldowns, and movement properties. Understanding when to cycle through these forms is the key to mastering both high-level boss fights and fast-paced PvP matches.

In Base Form, your skills focus on medium-range poking and crowd control. When you transition into Gear 2, your attacks gain massive speed buffs and altered animations, allowing you to execute lightning-fast combos. Gear 4 Boundman completely overrides your basic kit, turning you into an airborne juggernaut capable of breaking blocks and dealing massive area-of-effect damage.

| Move Name | Base Effect | Gear 2 Enhancement |
|--- |--- |--- |
| **Gum Pistol** | Single-target straight punch with medium knockback | Faster execution, reduced startup frames, added burn |
| **Gum Gatling** | Multi-hit rapid punches in a forward cone | Increased hit count, wider AOE, and higher shield pressure |
| **Gum Bazooka** | Double-palm strike dealing high knockback | Added shield-break capability and higher base damage |
| **Gum Rocket** | Pulls the player toward a target point or terrain | Faster travel velocity and instant startup |

When you progress to the late game and acquire the Gear 4 transformation, your moveset upgrades to the Boundman kit. This form consumes stamina continuously but provides unmatched offensive pressure.

| Skill Name | Description | Combat Application |
|--- |--- |--- |
| **Kong Gun** | Compressed high-impact punch | Primary burst damage and instant block breaker |
| **Rhino Schneider** | Flying double-kick from above | Excellent gap closer and AOE combat initiator |
| **Culverin** | Tracking directional punch | Ideal for catching dodging opponents in PvP |
| **Kong Organ** | Rapid-fire massive AOE barrage | Best for clearing boss groups and massive PvE mobs |

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Management">
Gear 4 Boundman drains your stamina bar rapidly. If your stamina hits zero, you will be forced back into Base Form and left temporarily vulnerable. Always monitor your stamina or pair the build with stamina-boosting races.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Map} title="How to Obtain the Haze Seas Gum Fruit and Gear 4 Book">

Acquiring the **haze seas gum fruit** and fully upgrading it to its Gear 4 state is a multi-step journey that spans from the early seas to the highest-level islands. There are three primary methods to obtain the base fruit: random map spawns, the in-game Gacha system, or checking the live stock.

Once you have secured the base fruit, your next major milestone is unlocking the Gear 4 Boundman transformation. This requires you to reach the late game, travel to Bubble Island, and defeat a powerful boss to drop a specific progression item.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain the Base Gum Fruit">
    You can roll the fruit via the Gacha NPC using Cash or Gems, purchase it when it appears in the live stock, or hunt for spawns. Fruits spawn under random trees across the map every 60 minutes and despawn after 20 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Reach Level 2000">
    Before attempting to farm the Gear 4 upgrade, you must level up your character. The G4 Boss is highly durable and deals immense damage, making Level 2000 the recommended minimum to fight him efficiently.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Travel to Bubble Island">
    Bubble Island is located in the late-game sector of the First Sea. Ensure your spawn point is set nearby, as you will likely need to defeat the boss multiple times to secure the drop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat the G4 Boss">
    Locate and challenge the G4 Boss on Bubble Island. He utilizes the full Gear 4 Boundman moveset, so keep your distance during his Kong Organ barrage and strike during his skill cooldowns.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Use the Gear 4 Book">
    The G4 Boss has a chance to drop the rare **Gear 4 Book**. Once obtained, consume the book while you have the Gum Fruit equipped to permanently unlock the Boundman transformation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="VIP Server Advantage">
If you are hunting for raw fruit spawns, utilize a VIP server. VIP servers have a strict 5-fruit spawn limit, allowing you to run clean farming routes without competition from other players.
</Callout>

</Section>
