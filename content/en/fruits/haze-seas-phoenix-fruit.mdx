<Section icon={Shield} title="Phoenix vs. Other Legendary Fruits">

Understanding how the **haze seas phoenix fruit** compares to its peers is essential for deciding whether to keep it or reroll. While Dough and Dragon offer higher raw DPS, the Phoenix provides utility that is unmatched in team-based gameplay and long-duration boss fights.

- **Phoenix vs. Dragon**: Dragon has higher AoE damage and transformation armor, but Phoenix has superior self-healing. Dragon is better for clearing waves; Phoenix is better for not dying to bosses.
- **Phoenix vs. Dough**: Dough is the king of PvP combos and stuns. Phoenix acts as the hard counter to Dough by out-sustaining the burst damage if the Phoenix user can survive the initial stun chain.
- **Phoenix vs. Magma**: Magma is the best for Sea Events (Sea Beasts). Phoenix is usable but lacks the high water-based damage modifiers that Magma possesses.

<Callout type="tip" title="Final Verdict">
Keep the Phoenix fruit if you value a consistent, low-stress gameplay experience where you rarely have to worry about your health bar. Reroll only if you are chasing the absolute highest DPS for speed-running raids.
</Callout>
