<Section icon={Map} title="Overview of the haze seas tremor fruit">

The **haze seas tremor fruit** (classically known as the Quake or Gura Gura no Mi) is a highly coveted Rare-rarity Paramecia-type fruit. Inspired by the legendary powers of Whitebeard, this fruit allows players to manipulate shockwaves, shatter the air, and summon devastating tsunamis to decimate entire fields of enemies. While it sits in the Rare tier alongside fruits like Buddha and Gas, its raw offensive capabilities rival even legendary and mythical choices in PvE scenarios.

Unlike elemental Logia fruits, the Tremor Fruit does not grant passive elemental immunity (Logia dodge). However, it compensates with some of the highest base damage modifiers in the game and unmatched crowd control. It is an exceptional tool for mid-game progression, boss farming, and clearing large groups of standard NPCs.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Detailed Skill Showcase & Mastery">

Unlocking the full potential of the Tremor Fruit requires leveling up your Fruit Mastery. Fortunately, the mastery requirements are incredibly forgiving, allowing you to unlock its ultimate screen-clearing move at Fruit Level 120. Each skill in the moveset serves a specific tactical purpose, ranging from close-quarters defense to massive ranged bombardment.

The moveset is designed to flow seamlessly into devastating combos, keeping enemies staggered and unable to retaliate. Below is the complete moveset breakdown, including the mastery levels required to unlock each ability.

| Move | Key | Mastery | Description | Rating |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Tremor Smash** | Z | Level 30 | Stomp the ground to unleash a concentrated shockwave | ★★★☆☆ |
| **Shock Ball** | X | Level 60 | Create and throw a ranged orb of kinetic energy | ★★★★☆ |
| **Tremor Launch** | C | Level 90 | Smash the earth to launch all surrounding enemies into the air | ★★★★☆ |
| **Quake Tsunami** | V | Level 120 | Summon two massive tsunamis that crash forward from the sea | ★★★★★ |

### Z Skill: Tremor Smash
The Tremor Smash is your bread-and-butter starter move. By stomping the ground, you release a localized shockwave that deals heavy damage to any enemies standing directly in front of you. It is highly effective for close-quarters self-defense and knocking back aggressive melee mobs.

### X Skill: Shock Ball
Shock Ball provides the Tremor Fruit with much-needed ranged utility. You summon a glowing sphere of kinetic energy and hurl it forward. Upon contact with an enemy or the terrain, the ball detonates in a large explosion, dealing area damage and interrupting enemy attacks.

### C Skill: Tremor Launch
Tremor Launch is one of the best combo starters in Haze Seas. You strike the ground with immense force, fracturing the air and launching all nearby enemies high into the sky. This move is perfect for grouping up scattered mobs or setting up a follow-up air combo with your sword or fighting style.

### V Skill: Quake Tsunami
The crown jewel of the Tremor Fruit is the Quake Tsunami. When activated, your character charges up and shakes the entire sea, summoning two colossal waves that sweep across the battlefield. This skill has a massive area of effect and can easily wipe out entire camps of NPCs in a single cast.

<Callout type="warning" title="Tsunami Vulnerability">
The Quake Tsunami (V skill) requires a 2 to 3-second charge-up time before activation. Because you do not get invincibility frames (i-frames) during this animation, you are highly vulnerable to being hit, interrupted, or killed by high-level bosses if you charge it too close to them. Always execute this move from a safe distance.
</Callout>
