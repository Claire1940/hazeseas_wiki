<Section icon={Swords} title="Operation Fruit Moveset & Abilities">

To make the most of the Operation fruit, players must understand its unique mechanical limitation: the Room. While inside the active zone, you can manipulate the environment, swap places with enemies to disrupt their positioning, and deal high bursts of elemental and physical damage.

The table below outlines the complete moveset for the Operation fruit in Haze Seas, detailing the keybinds, stat requirements, and the functional behavior of each ability.

| Skill Name | Keybind | Stat Requirement | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Room** | E | 1 Fruit Stat | Creates a spherical zone required for all skills |
| **Takt** | Z | 30 Fruit Stat | Smashes the ground at the cursor |
| **Levitation** | X | 60 Fruit Stat | Breaks and smashes ground elements together |
| **Shambles** | C | 90 Fruit Stat | Teleports and swaps places, dealing swap damage |
| **Gamma Knife** | V | 120 Fruit Stat | Deals a massive slash of concentrated energy |
| **Puncture** | B / H | 150 Fruit Stat | Charges up to 15 slashes with purple explosions |

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Warning">
If you step outside the boundaries of your active Room, or if the Room's duration expires, all other offensive skills will immediately become unavailable until a new Room is cast.
</Callout>

Takt and Levitation serve as your primary farming tools. By pulling groups of enemies into your Room, you can chain these two abilities together to quickly clear out waves of mobs without taking direct damage. Shambles acts as an excellent defensive tool, allowing you to swap places with an attacking enemy to dodge high-damage boss skills while simultaneously dealing minor damage.

<Section icon={Map} title="How to Get the Operation Blade">

While the Operation fruit is incredibly potent on its own, it achieves its true potential when paired with the Operation Blade. This sword, modeled after Trafalgar Law's Kikoku, offers a complementary moveset that scales well into the mid-to-late game. Obtaining this sword requires players to brave the volcanic hazards of Half Hot / Half Cold Island.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Meet the Requirements">
    Ensure your character is at least Level 1000+ and you have at least $250,000 Beli saved in your in-game wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to Half Hot / Half Cold Island">
    Travel to the Half Hot / Half Cold Island, which is marked as a Level 1450 zone on your Haze Seas map.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Magma Cliff Cave">
    Scale the cliffs on the volcanic (Half Hot) side of the island. Look for a small cave opening hidden along the rocky walls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Purchase from the Magma Minion">
    Enter the cave, speak to the Magma Minion NPC, and pay the $250,000 Beli fee to unlock the Operation Blade.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Weapon Synergy">
The Operation Blade features two powerful sword skills that complement your fruit abilities, allowing you to weave sword strikes and telekinetic attacks together.
